# Institutional Deal Pipeline Blueprint

## 1) Pipeline Philosophy
UCH targets **aligned institutional buyers** who want **ownership/control**, not SaaS lock‑in.

## 2) Target Tiers
### Tier 1 — Regional Private Hospital Groups
- Deal size: $75K–$250K
- Sales cycle: 4–6 months
- Decision makers: CIO/CTO/Medical Director
- Regions: MENA, East Africa, SEA

### Tier 2 — Government Digital Health Programs
- Deal size: $250K–$350K+
- Sales cycle: 6–12 months
- Decision makers: Ministry of Health / Digital Transformation

### Tier 3 — Health‑Tech Startups
- Deal size: $75K–$150K
- Sales cycle: 2–4 months
- Decision makers: Founder/CTO

## 3) First 5 Buyer Archetypes
1. Gulf hospital group (5–10 hospitals) modernizing teleconsultation
2. MENA government pilot for national telehealth expansion
3. African regional hospital network digitizing outpatient services
4. VC-backed telehealth startup lacking backend infrastructure
5. Insurance-backed health network expanding remote services

## 4) Acquisition Phases
- Months 1–3: warm access + demos (goal 5–8 active conversations)
- Months 3–6: paid pilot offering (goal close 1 ownership or 2 license)
- Months 6–12: reference-led expansion (goal 3–5 closings)

## 5) Funnel Math (Conservative)
To close **1 Ownership** deal:
- ~10 serious conversations
- ~3 formal proposals
- 1–2 exec-level demos

## 6) Discipline Metrics
Track monthly:
- Conversations opened
- NDAs signed
- Demos delivered
- Proposals submitted
- Pilot agreements executed
- Close probability
