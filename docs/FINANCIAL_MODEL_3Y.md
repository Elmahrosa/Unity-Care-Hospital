# 3‑Year Financial Model (Framework)

**Purpose:** conservative, milestone-linked revenue model for institutional buyers.

---

## Pricing Tiers (Reference)
| Tier | Price | Buyer |
|---|---:|---|
| License | $75K | Regional hospital groups / operators |
| Ownership Transfer | $250K | Private operator / strategic buyer |
| Sovereign Stack | $350K | Government / national program |

Maintenance/support: typically **15–20%** of license value annually (optional; attach rate target **70%**).

---

## Deployment Pace Assumptions
Institutional sales cycles are slower and relationship-driven.

### Year 1 (Pilot)
- 2 License deals = $150K
- 1 Ownership transfer = $250K
**Total:** $400K

### Year 2 (Expansion)
- 6 License deals = $450K
- 2 Ownership transfers = $500K
- 1 Sovereign stack = $350K
**Total:** $1.30M

### Year 3 (Scaling)
- 12 License deals = $900K
- 4 Ownership transfers = $1.00M
- 2 Sovereign stacks = $700K
**Total:** $2.60M

---

## Recurring Revenue Layer
With 70% attach rate and 15–20% maintenance:
- Year 3 maintenance ARR estimate: **$600K–$800K**
- Total Year 3 revenue estimate: **$3.2M–$3.4M**

---

## Lean Expense Model (Post‑Seed)
Annual operating costs (conservative):
- 2 Engineers: $120K
- 1 Sales lead: $70K
- Compliance/audit prep: $60K
- Cloud: $40K
- Legal/admin: $30K

**Total:** $320K–$400K/year

---

## Break‑Even Logic
Break‑even in **Year 2** if ≥8 institutional deals close (mix dependent).

---

## Valuation Anchors
Infrastructure + enterprise software comps often price at:
- **3x–6x ARR** (conservative)
- **6x–10x ARR** (growth)

If Year 3 ARR = $3.0M (conservative):
- Low: $9M
- Mid: $15M
- High: $24M
