# Due Diligence Defense Document (Board/Investor Version)

**Project:** Unity Care Hospital (UCH)

**Positioning:** *Institutional-grade digital health infrastructure* — deployable as a licensed white‑label platform or as a full ownership transfer. UCH is designed for institutional buyers who require control, deployment isolation, and extensibility (rather than consumer SaaS lock‑in).

---

## 1) What UCH Is
- **Modular telehealth infrastructure** for private hospital groups, health operators, and sovereign digital health programs.
- **Deployment as an asset**: licensing, ownership transfer, or sovereign deployment model.
- **Audit‑ready posture**: security-by-design, governance-first documentation, and evidence packaging.

## 2) What Is Live vs. Roadmap
### Live / MVP (current)
- Teleconsultation workflows
- WebRTC video consultation
- Role-based access control (RBAC) foundation
- Secure records storage (encrypted-at-rest assumptions, access logging)
- Dedicated deployment approach (per-institution isolation)

### Roadmap (milestone-gated)
- **Blockchain notarization** module (immutability/attestation layer)
- **IoT vitals** integration layer
- **Insurance API** abstraction
- **AI triage** assistant (clinical decision support, not diagnosis)

> **Rule:** we do **not** claim modules as “live” unless they are implemented and demonstrable in the repo or deployed environment.

---

## 3) Technical Architecture Summary
- **Backend:** Node.js
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **API layer:** GraphQL
- **Deployment:** containerized, horizontally scalable, cloud-native
- **Security controls:** RBAC, request logging, secure secret handling via environment variables

---

## 4) Compliance & Security Posture
- **Compliance posture:** HIPAA-aligned architecture; SOC 2 principles applied in design.
- **Certification status:** Formal certification audits are **scheduled post‑seed funding**.
- **Data governance:** Institution retains data ownership; deployments are isolated; no centralized data extraction.

---

## 5) Commercial Model (Institutional)
**Pricing tiers (reference):**
- **License:** $75K+
- **Ownership transfer:** $250K+
- **Sovereign stack:** $350K+

**Recurring revenue:** maintenance/support typically **15–20% of license value** (optional; attach rate target 70%).

---

## 6) Roadmap & Execution Discipline
- Milestone-based roadmap with clear scope boundaries.
- Pilot → evaluation → conversion path (license/ownership/sovereign).
- Investor funds mapped to: hardening, compliance evidence package, and institutional pipeline execution.

---

## 7) Risk Register (Honest)
- **Distribution risk:** long institutional sales cycles.
- **Regulatory risk:** payment rails differ by jurisdiction; crypto optional.
- **Execution risk:** audits require time and process maturity.

**Mitigations:** pilot structure with paid entry, modular rollout, fiat fallback, audit planning.

---

## 8) Evidence Package (What We Provide)
- Architecture overview
- Deployment and environment guide
- Compliance posture doc
- Hostile CTO audit notes + remediation log
- Proposal template (procurement/board)
- Financial model, cashflow forecast, and sensitivity analysis
